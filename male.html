Causes of Poor Sexual Performance in Men

Alcohol & Smoking

Excessive consumption of alcohol & smoking can reduce blood circulation and testosterone levels leading to erectile dysfunction.

Age

Men in their 40s-50s may notice weaker or less frequent erections since blood vessels lose elasticity overtime hence reducing blood flow to the penis.

Stress, Anxiety & Depression

Work, financial or personal stress can lower libido.

Depression affecs mood and sexual desire.

Lack of Exercise/Physical Activity

Lack of physical activity weakens stamina and affects blood flow.

Masturbation

Masturbation can have both positive and negative effects on sexual performance in men depending on frequency, mindset and overall lifestyle. Some of the negative effects of masturbation include:

Erectile Dysfunction- Oversimulation through frequent masturbation can desensitize nerve endings, making it harder to achieve or maintain an erection during real intimacy.

Delayed Ejaculation

The body may adapt to self simulation, making it difficult to climax during intercourse.

Decreased Libido

Excessive ejaculation can temporarily lower testosterone levels, reducing sexual desire.
